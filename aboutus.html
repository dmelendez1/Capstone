A dog named Chocomil

Meet Chocomil, the pickiest eater in the neighborhood. No matter how hard I tried, finding the right dog food for Max was like solving a puzzle with missing pieces. It seemed like Chocomil had an aversion to just about every dog food in the market. His discerning taste buds and sensitive stomach turned mealtime into a daily challenge for both Chocomil and his owner. We embarked on a culinary journey through countless brands, flavors, and formulations, desperately seeking a solution to Chocomil's food woes.

As a veterinarian it was clear that Chocomil needed a special solution tailored to his unique preferences and dietary needs. Drawing upon my expertise and a deep passion for pet health and happiness, I decided to take matters into my own hands. After extensive research and consultation with fellow pet nutrition experts, I set out to create a one-of-a-kind dog kibble, specially crafted for picky eaters like Chocomil. The goal was clear: to provide dogs with delicious and nutritious meals that would not only tantalize their taste buds but also be gentle on their sensitive stomachs. And so, the journey to develop the perfect dog kibble began, driven by a commitment to make mealtime a joyful experience for pets like Chocomil and their dedicated owners.

-Diana & The Kibble Kitchen Team

Our Mission

We have transformed our unwavering love for dogs into a simple yet profound commitment to their well-being, starting with the contents of their food bowls. Our journey began with a group passionate dog lover who had grown weary of the highly-processed, unappetizing offerings touted as 'natural' and 'healthy.' In response, they decided to revolutionize pet nutrition from the ground up.
